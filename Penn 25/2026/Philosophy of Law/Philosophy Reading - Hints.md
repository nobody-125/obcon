##### Workflow
Start by skimming the text. It doesn't matter if you don't understand everything at first, just try to get a generalized idea of what it's about. Take note of the examples that are used (if they exist). On the second read through, be more verbose; understand how each part of the text answers the key questions.
##### While Reading
- Keep in mind what the fundamental argument is.
- Contextualize who the audience of the text is, or how the text relates to other philosophical works.
- Internalize the arguments that the author uses.

Summarize the text at the end (we already do this).
