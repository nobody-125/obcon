### Seni di Indonesia II
The Indonesian arts and culture
##### Affandi (1907-1990)
* **Pelukis:** Painter
* Unique tube-squeezing technique, where paint is directly applied to the canvas
* Categorized as expressionist and created paintings of locations like Paris, but also made caricatures, portraits, etc:
	* "Dia Datang, Dia Minunggu, Dia Pergi" (1944): He comes, stays, goes
	* "Ibuku" (1944): My mother
##### S. Sudjojono
* Messy brushwork, muddy mixed-up colors
* Now known as the "Father of Modern Indonesian Painting"
* Submitted "Kinderen met Kat" (Children with Cat) in 1938 to the Bataviasche Kunstkring, a prestigious Dutch colonial art center, making him famous
* **Realisme nasi (Rice realism):** Sudjojono promoted an impressionistic, yet real style, that was digestible and satisfying, like rice. (Makan nasi, eating rice)
* **Seni lukis adalah jiwa kethok:** Art is a visible soul, every stroke says something about the artist.
##### ?
* Melukis di taman: Painting in the park, garden

##### PERSAGI
* Stands for the association of Indonesian Drawing Experts
* Created the modern Indonesian words in the visual arts, which had never existed before
	* Seni: Art
	* Seniman: Artist
	* Seni Lukis: Art of painting
	* Lukisan: Painting
	* Pelukis: Painter
	* Kampas: Canvas
	* Pematung: Sculptor
	* Seni rupa: Visual arts
	* Cukilan: Print
	* Alam benda: Still-life
* Sudjojono became the face of PERSAGI and defended the association, along with the Indonesian art movement, from Dutch critics
